Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01/01/2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how HabitChain handles user information. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

2. Information Collection

HabitChain does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App works entirely offline and does not require user accounts, login, or an internet connection. All habit data entered by the user is stored locally on the user’s device only.

3. Data Usage

Since no personal or sensitive data is collected, no data is processed on external servers, analyzed, or used for tracking or profiling.

4. Data Sharing

The App does not share any data with third parties, advertisers, analytics services, or external organizations.

5. Data Storage & Security

All user-entered data is stored locally on the device using secure local storage mechanisms. We do not have access to this data.

6. Children’s Privacy

HabitChain does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since the App does not collect data, there is no risk of children’s data being collected.

7. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party services that collect user data such as analytics, ads, or tracking SDKs.
